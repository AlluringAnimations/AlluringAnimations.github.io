About Me

Hello my name is Qara Thomas and I am Alluring Animations. My passion for animation is very deep rooted because I grew up loving animation. I have always loved drawing and I adored animation in every form, from kids shows to Anime, but never really thought of animating myself. It was about the time I entered High School when my passion shifted from drawing still pictures to making small little clips and movies. Now I love speed drawing and I love to animate as much as ever.
